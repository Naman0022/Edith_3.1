Justice League is a team of superheroes appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The team has been featured in various comic book series, animated television series, films, and video games. The team's roster has varied over the years, but some of its most iconic members include Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, The Flash, Green Lantern, Aquaman, and Martian Manhunter. The Justice League is dedicated to protecting the Earth from threats that are too great for any one superhero to handle alone.
